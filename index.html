What is uChews?

UChews provides a platform for busy Americans to find and swap healthy homemade meals with each other. For example, I can cook Chinese food, and you may be good at cooking Italian, but we don’t want to eat the same cuisine all the time, so let’s trade! Cooking is time consuming, and trying to prepare a completely new dish? Heh. Even worse. UChews enables users to save money and time by preparing extra portions, packing them into provided tupperware, and then meeting up with each other to exchange meals.
